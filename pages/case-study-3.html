The start

The project for BAK Logistics took place on the biggest cross academy hackathon organized by Brainster. We worked in a team of 3 people for UX/UI, 3 for Graphic Design and 2 for Marketing. After the initiation the clock started ticking 72 hours to take this emerging start up logistics company and do a total strategy with a responsive web site. We firstly started with coordinating the 3 separate teams, to establish a good communication, and work together, so the whole project has a good, cohesive flow. After the stakeholder interview, we got to know each other, divided our tasks and got to work.

The process

The main conclusion from the stakeholder interview is where we could explore the problem we needed to solve. BAK Logistics has given us the task to design a brand-focused platform where their clients can learn more about the services they offer. Their website needs to exhibit their true values â€“ reliable, secure and trustworthy. In order for clients to find them, they need a good online presence, considering they have options on the market. We researched the logistics market to highlight the must have functionalities. It was really important to the stakeholder to have a good landing page, animated hero section, to draw attention to their clients. We were really focused to do a top-notch high-fidelity prototype to meet the needs of this start up.

The Product

We did it! In 72 hours with a lot of focus and devotion, tons of coffee, very little sleep, and good division of the tasks, we managed to do a full responsive website with a sleek, corporate yet stand-out design. The details were very important. In all the photos featured on the website, we made sure that we have the BAK Logistics logo, to reflect the ambitions and goals of the company, and a get done attitude. On the landing page the logo has changing pictures with the different types of transportation and storage solution, where you can feel the dynamic of the logistics market. Check out the full prototype to get the real feel.

The Lesson

This project was the best lesson I had in the whole academy. It has deepened my understanding of the role of a UX/UI designer. Good communication is critical between the different departments, and it reflects in the final product.
